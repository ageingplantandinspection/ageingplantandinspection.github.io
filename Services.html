OUR SERVICES

CONSULTING & PROJECT MANAGEMENT RELATING TO BUILT INFRASTRUCTURE  

Project management of construction projects with a particular focus on remedial work to existing ageing infrastructure.
Tendering and specification for new construction, civil upgrades and refurbishment. New construction & refurbishment design within industrial and COMAH environments.

Setup of RBI and inspection plans for estates, built infrastructure and piping systems. 

 Contractor management within COMAH environments.

 Assessment of site civil infrastructure suitability focusing on safety. (Slips, trips, falls, falling objects, ageing structures).

DRAFTING SERVICES

We provide a wide range of drafting services to assist the operational, inspection and project needs of our clients.
Services include the creation and modification of the following:

Site plans.
Elevation drawings.
P&IDs.
Pipework isometrics.
Inspection plans.
Site access and safety drawings.
Isolation drawings.
Exclusion zone drawings.

INSPECTION & ASSET MANAGEMENT (BUILT ENVIRONMENT & PIPING SYSTEMS)

We provide civil infrastructure and pipework inspection services as follows:

Documenting of built assets and pipework. (Site asset registers, location plans, defining boundary limits, asset cataloguing, RBI).

Inspection plans and reporting. (Defect assessment, priority, NDT requirements, specialist requirements,  remedial recommendations).

Visual inspection and reporting on existing insitu and in-service built infrastructure and piping systems (structures, buildings, bunds, ASME B31.3 piping to API 570).
